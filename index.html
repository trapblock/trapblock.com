FAQ

Common Questions

Do you block licensed or regulated casinos?
No. We only block the fake websites that scam casino ads use to get around platform safety checks. Real, licensed gambling services are not affected. The problem we are solving is not gambling. It is people pretending to offer it while actually running a scam. If a business is legitimate and licensed, TrapBlock will not touch it.
Does TrapBlock work inside TikTok, YouTube, Instagram, and Google?
Yes. Most ad blockers only work inside your browser. TrapBlock works at the iPhone level, so it protects you inside every app, including TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, Facebook, and Google. When a fake ad tries to load, it gets stopped before any app can open it.
Does it slow down my internet?
No. In most cases it actually makes your browsing slightly faster because it skips connections to known scam sites instantly instead of waiting for them to load. You will not notice any difference in your everyday use.
What if a legitimate website gets blocked by mistake?
Email us at support@trapblock.com with the domain and we'll review it within 24 hours. Confirmed legitimate sites are added to the permanent whitelist and never blocked again.
Does it work on Android and Windows?
Android and Windows support is coming soon. Right now TrapBlock is available for iPhone and iPad. We started with iOS because iPhone users are the primary target of fake casino ad scams — scammers know iOS users trust both the App Store and the ad platforms, which makes them the most valuable audience to deceive.
What is iCloud Private Relay and does it affect TrapBlock?
iCloud Private Relay is an Apple feature that hides your browsing from some trackers. When it is on, it can sometimes get in the way of TrapBlock's protection inside Safari. We recommend turning it off for full coverage: Settings > [Your Name] > iCloud > Private Relay > Off. Every other app on your phone stays protected regardless.
Why can't TikTok, Google, or the App Store catch these scams themselves?
Scammers register a fresh fake website for each ad campaign. When TikTok or Google's safety system scans the ad, that website looks completely clean. Only real users get sent to the fake casino. By the time the platforms catch on, the campaign has already run. Scammers then register a new website and start again. TrapBlock catches these sites by recognising their patterns, not their history, so it works even on ones that have never been seen before.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Cancel from your dashboard at any time. Your protection stays active until the end of your billing period. No cancellation fees.
